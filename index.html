A Neighborhood Resurgence in the Making ...

Fishtown & Kensington, Philadelphia

Fishtown / Kensington, Philadelphia is a vibrant neighborhood experiencing tremendous growth and resurgence. The artistic and culinary communities have embraced this working class area that despite a strong community has recently had some trouble with the opiod epidemic and are quickly turning it into one of Philadelphia's most interesting and exciting places to live, work and play.

Great Food & Drink

Fishtown has been the focus of national magazines touting it's culinary scene and for good reason ... it has some of the best restaurants in the city and any new ones looking to make a mark are headed here....just a few of the best known are:

  • WM Mulherin's
  • Heffe Tacos...tacos that don't suck.
  • Suraya

Variety

Fishtown has all sorts of cool stuff. It's sometimes known for being home to "hipsters" (and yes, they're here) but that mainly means you'll find the following:

  1. Strong sense of community and activism.
  2. Small stores and artisinal shops.
  3. Dog Parks
  4. Just a friendly vibe.
